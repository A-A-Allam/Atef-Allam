PROFESSIONAL JOURNEY

My Career

A comprehensive overview of a distinguished 45-year career in vascular surgery, medical education, and pioneering endovascular techniques in Egypt. From house officer to emeritus professor, a journey of continuous innovation and academic excellence.

A distinguished 45-year journey in vascular surgery, from introducing endovascular techniques to Egypt in 1998, to establishing the first independent vascular surgery department, and training over 30 vascular surgeons who now lead departments across the country.
