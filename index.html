About

Pioneer of Endovascular Surgery

Professor Atef Allam introduced endovascular surgery to Egypt in 1998, training over 30 vascular surgeons and establishing the first duplex-guided intervention program in the country.

Founding Leader

Co-founder and first Secretary General of the Vascular Society of Egypt (2004), later serving as President (2013-2015). Supervised 50+ M.D. and M.S. thesis across multiple universities.

International Recognition

Johns Hopkins University Research Fellow (1994-1995), presented at 60+ international conferences, and established Egypt as a leader in duplex-guided endovascular procedures.

A Distinguished Medical Career

Born December 17, 1954, Professor Atef Abdel Galil Mohamed Allam has dedicated over four decades to advancing vascular and endovascular surgery in Egypt and internationally. As Emeritus Professor at Al-Azhar University Faculty of Medicine for Girls, he stands as a pioneer in endovascular surgery and a founding leader of organized vascular medicine in Egypt.
